Welcome to DOrSU SmartMap System

Your navigational guide to the Davao Oriental State University Main Campus.

Our Services

The DOrSU Main Campus SmartMap System is a comprehensive navigation and information system designed to guide students, staff, and visitors seamlessly across the campus grounds. Its core services center around providing location tracking and efficient, step-by-step routing to various points of interest, including specific buildings, classrooms, amenities, and University Admin. Beyond navigation, the app enhances campus life by offering a searchable directory of locations, potentially integrating information about ongoing campus events, and utilizing visual, user-friendly interfaces to ensure ease of use and improved accessibility for everyone navigating the Davao Oriental State University Main Campus.
